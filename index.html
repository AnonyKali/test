Domain Valuation FAQs

What is a 5L or 6L domain?

A 5L domain is a 5-letter .com domain name, while a 6L domain is a 6-letter .com domain. These are considered premium domains due to their brevity and memorability.

How accurate are your domain valuations?

Our domain appraisal algorithm analyzes thousands of historical sales to provide the most accurate estimates for auction, marketplace, and brokerage valuations.
